About Us




We are a Professional Engineering Corporation who offer Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing (MEP) services with a specialty in Fire Protection, Fire Alarm, ARCS.

Tower Consulting Engineers offers full engineering design and consulting services throughout the city of New York. Our qualified Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing engineers are exceedingly capable of designing reliable and cost-effective systems. From tenant buildouts or high-rise buildings to new and retrofit construction our staff is available every step of the process. We specialize in creating a full package plan for clients undertaking any actions with the Fire Department of New York.
